Book Collection

Your book collection! Your wishlist, collection, favorate books whatever you want!

Create a collection of books with the "Add Book" button! Make sure you add a book cover! Just find an image of the book online, right click on the image and select "copy image adress" and past it in the book url input.

To manage your book collection, hover over your books to access your options on each book.

"Info" will send you to the amazon page for your book or select "Delete" to remove the selected book from your collection.
